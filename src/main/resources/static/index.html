General Rule

  • Stock must be hedged, using hedging stock, shorting, gold, oil or other methods.
  • For long term position, holding value stocking might be fine in long run, but need to watch out financial reports to prevent pivots.
  • Shorting and options are having risks. Need to watch out the environments all the time.
  • Pay close attention to political changes, wars and public health issues. Even making less gain it is ok, better cash out if there is a chance.
  • Research in ETF, Bonds, Forex or foreign market stocks for diversities.
  • Crisis normally indicates opportunities, however, prepare cash for it before hand.
  • Short term trading gain is debatable but in my theory it compounds fast depending on how you act.

Specific Rule for Short Term

Time management

  • Sell in high rising day, djia goes 100-400 higher for instance, and buy it in low days.
  • Trading time matters, selling it at opening is fine, or before 9:45 AM, best to be around 10:00 AM. Or short sell during this time. As dump money and people's hype is high at 10:00 and going down after 10:30 AM, so buy after 10:30.
  • In a big sales day, when index drops a few hundreds, watch closely around 10:00 to 10:30 AM. Don't go earlier than that. And watch to buy more during 3:00-4:00 PM. As panic goes in and peak at 10:30 AM.
  • Friday is normally a good day.
  • Don't trade if you are tiring, sick or not feeling well.
    Rest for a few days and good result may come.
    Keep reading and study.

Fund management

  • Before any crisis, cash out, and watch for opportunities. Better not to buy anything. Don't go to buy mutual funds. Mutual funds depends on the stock price as well, it is not going to hedge anything.
  • When stock price is at historically high, and shorting is at high risk too, then need to check out gold stock and bonds.
  • At any buy day, keep your funds half in cash or margin balance in case you made some mistake to save the positions.
  • When your research is not confident, don't buy.
  • When you don't want to trade, study but don't waste your time.
  • If you really want to buy and you are not sure, buy 10 shares, if it turns out good, then you can fill out more.
  • Don't put the heavy load in one buy, buy in batches, batch side depends on the stock price and possibility to go up or down.
    Batch size:
    stock price 20 or less: size 100.
    stock price around 20-50: size 50.
    stock price around 50-100: size 25.
    stock price over 100: size 20.
    stock price close to 300: size 10.
    stock price close over 500: size 3.

Adjust positions

  • Adjust position can be risky, note that if you are seeking the price in half way of your price and the target price, you will have to double your investment and shares. That is geometric sequence, which is 2, 4, 8, 16. You will need to read chart and do a prediction before you just double down.
  • In any case where you don't have funds anymore and you want to save your stocks, you will need to manually stop limit by getting rid of of some shares, get money back and reinvest. I don't do automatic stop limit but do it manually as automatic can be more risker.
  • Short more in a big plunging day, and the momentum and news is very positive for you.
  • Buy more in a big rising day.
Note: The above looks not normal for many people. Note that buy more in a big rising day, so it can easily pass the price you bought. And this buy is to save your position, not looking for a under-valued opportunity. For looking to buy and make a fortunate, we need to wait to buy in a day where there was big blood on the street, which is near the bottom of a crisis.
Likewise, you need to short more and strong when it is a big dropping day, and it has the possibility to drop below the price you shorted.

Shorting

  • Do not short if the current price is lower than the open price, there is a high possibility it could reverse. The chance to win is only 50%.
  • Short a stock that is historical high and there is no reason it will stay there and go up forever.
  • Try to short traditional stocks for less risks. High tech stocks tends to have a lot of irrational.
  • In rare cases where the market is unreasonable high, short more than buy more.
  • One indication for big market crash is that oil price is dropping and gold/silver is high. If interest rate and bond market can not tell you that, oil price plunge before the market crash. Get your ammo and ready for a big crash, buy in batch.
